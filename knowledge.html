Consumer Rights Guide

Your three‑step roadmap to grievance redressal in India
1

Internal Resolution

Always start with the company's official customer support. Use their app, email, or helpline. Keep a record of your Ticket ID or complaint number. Most companies resolve issues at this stage.

2

Nodal / Grievance Officer

If you don't get a satisfactory response within 15‑30 days, escalate to the company's Nodal Officer or Grievance Redressal Officer. Their contact details are usually on the company's website under “Grievance”.

3

Statutory Regulator

If the company still fails to respond, approach the sector‑specific regulator: RBI Ombudsman (banking), IRDAI (insurance), TRAI (telecom), DGCA (aviation), FSSAI (food safety), or the National Consumer Helpline (1915). Use our draft generator to create a formal complaint.
